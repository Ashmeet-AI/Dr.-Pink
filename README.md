# Psycho-Creative Community Web Application

A web-based community platform focused on emotional expression, creativity, and authentic human connection.  
The core of the platform is built around **art, music, writing, and emotions** as primary languages of expression.

This is not a typical social network. It is designed as a calm, safe space where people can create, feel, and share without performance pressure or algorithm-driven engagement.

---

## Purpose

The platform helps users:
- Express emotions through creative mediums
- Engage with others through reflection rather than debate
- Build a gentle rhythm of community participation
- Experience creativity as a tool for self-awareness and healing

---

## Key Features

### Creative Expression
- Share artwork, music/audio, and written reflections
- Optional emotion tagging on every post
- Private drafts and personal journaling
- Anonymous sharing for writing when needed

### Community & Interaction
- Community feed with mixed creative content
- Gentle reactions (beyond likes)
- Threaded comments focused on reflection
- Topic-based forums and discussions
- Small group circles for deeper connection

### Emotional Presence
- Daily emotion check-ins
- Private emotional timeline
- Ability to share emotional states with the community
- Emotion-aware responses and reactions

### Engagement & Rhythm
- Weekly themes and creative invitations
- Calm, non-urgent notifications
- Saved posts and collections
- Background audio listening for music content

### Safety & Trust
- Trauma-aware moderation
- Sensitive content warnings
- Privacy and visibility controls
- Gentle reporting and support flows

---

- Expression before explanation  
- Creation over consumption  
- Emotional presence over performance  
- Community as witnessing, not validation
